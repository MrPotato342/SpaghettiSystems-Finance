Understanding Financial Independence

FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) focuses on building enough invested assets to sustain long-term living expenses without relying on active employment income.

This planner estimates your required retirement corpus using the widely used 4% withdrawal framework, inflation-adjusted future expenses, and long-term investment growth assumptions.
